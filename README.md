# Image Resizer & Compressor

A simple, fast, and secure web-based tool to resize images and compress them to a target file size. Everything happens locally in your browser—**no images are ever uploaded to a server**, ensuring your privacy and security.

## Features
*   **Custom Dimensions:** Specify your own maximum width and height.
*   **Automatic Resizing:** Maintains the original aspect ratio while resizing.
*   **Targeted Compression:** Specify a maximum file size (in KB) and the tool will automatically adjust the image quality to hit that target.
*   **Format Conversion:** Automatically converts images (PNG, WebP, etc.) to JPEG format.
*   **Privacy First:** Zero server-side processing. All calculations are performed on your local device.

## How to Use
1.  Open the [Live Site](https://your-github-username.github.io/your-repo-name/) in your web browser.
2.  **Upload Image:** Click the button to select an image from your device.
3.  **Set Dimensions:** Enter your preferred **Max Width** and **Max Height** in pixels.
4.  **Set Size:** Enter your **Target Max File Size** in KB.
5.  **Process:** Click the **Resize & Compress** button.
6.  **Download:** Once the process is complete, click the **Download Image** button to save your new file.
